This guide contains everything you need for a successful Product Hunt launch, including copy, assets, timing strategy, and engagement tactics.

## ðŸ“… Launch Timeline

### 2 Weeks Before Launch

**Build anticipation and gather support:**

You should start building momentum on Twitter and other platforms to ensure you have supporters ready to upvote on launch day. Product Hunt rewards early engagement, so having people ready to upvote in the first hour is critical.

**Actions:**
- Create Product Hunt profile if you don't have one
- Join Product Hunt community and engage with other launches
- Connect with 20+ "hunters" (people who frequently launch products)
- Prepare all launch assets (screenshots, videos, copy)
- Build email list of supporters
- Announce launch date on Twitter

### 1 Week Before Launch

**Final preparations:**

This is when you finalize all your materials and do a dry run to ensure everything is perfect. You only get one shot at Product Hunt, so preparation is key.

**Actions:**
- Finalize product description and tagline
- Record and edit demo video
- Take high-quality screenshots
- Write first comment (crucial for engagement)
- Prepare responses to common questions
- Schedule social media posts
- Email your list about upcoming launch

### Launch Day (Tuesday-Thursday recommended)

**Execute your launch plan:**

Product Hunt resets at 12:01 AM PST. Launching at this exact time gives you the full 24 hours to accumulate upvotes. Avoid launching on weekends or Mondays when engagement is lower.

**Timeline:**
- **12:01 AM PST**: Submit product to Product Hunt
- **12:05 AM PST**: Post first comment with additional context
- **12:10 AM PST**: Share on Twitter, LinkedIn, email list
- **Throughout the day**: Respond to every comment within 30 minutes
- **Peak hours** (9 AM - 5 PM PST): Be extra active
- **End of day**: Thank supporters and share results

### Post-Launch (Days 2-7)

**Maintain momentum:**

Even after the 24-hour launch window, you can continue to drive traffic and signups. Many users discover products days after the initial launch through Product Hunt's weekly newsletters and collections.

**Actions:**
- Share launch results on social media
- Thank everyone who supported
- Collect and implement feedback
- Continue engaging with comments
- Reach out to Product Hunt curators for featuring
- Submit to other directories (BetaList, Hacker News, etc.)
